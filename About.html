✨ABOUT US! ✨

OUR HISTORY!📜

Founded in 2025, JJ PLV began as a shared idea among a group of students who were constantly frustrated by the lack of affordable, high-quality, and aesthetic stationery. We realized that the tools we used every day to study and create were either too expensive or lacked personality. We believe that putting pen to paper is a magical experience. Our little shop started from a simple obsession with finding some fun stationary items that are affordable. What started as a small project to design the perfect, most functional stationary quickly grew into a full-fledged business. Today, JJ PLV is proud to offer a carefully curated range of stationery designed to help you stay organized, focused, and inspired

Whether you are bullet journaling, writing letters to friends, or just trying to keep your desk organized, we carefully curate and design products to bring a little joy to your everyday tasks.

OUR MISSION!🎯

To banish boring desks and inspire your next big idea. At JJ PLV, we don't just sell notebooks and pens,we provide the ultimate tools for self-expression, ultimate productivity, and aesthetic focus. We curate high-quality, ultra-functional, and fun stationery that makes you actually want to sit down, study, create, and smash your goals without breaking the bank.TRUST US, you will have the best time using these stationeries!!

OUR VISION!🔮

  • Quality ✒️: Premium, resistant to bleeding and feathering, guaranteed smooth writing.
  • Sustainability 🌱:Eco-friendly stationery delivered in 100% recyclable, plastic-free packaging.
  • Creativity 🎨 :Aesthetic and colourful stationery crafted to inspire your everyday ideas.
